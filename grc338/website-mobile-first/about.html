About

Our Story

Honeycomb Cafe is a family owend cafe. We pride ourselves in our simple, yet flavorfull menu that keeps customers coming back! We started as a very small shop located in Santa Barbara, California, and grew over the past 15 years to have a total of 3 locations. We would like to thank our loyal customers for their unbelieveable support. We would not be here without your endless help. Due to the circumstances surrounding COVID-19, we are only oding take-out orders and outside dining. If you have any questions, please give us a call!
